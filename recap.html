Three Paths to Excel:

  1. Head-to-Head Matches
  2. Judged Awards
  3. Skill Runs
Matches / Skills

Head-to-Head Matches: 2v2

  • Autonomous: For the first 30 seconds, robots are programmed to run on their own to complete tasks.
  • Driver Control: For the last 1 minute 30 seconds, robots are controlled by drivers.
  • Each team completes 10 qualifier matches within their division of 40 teams.
  • The top 16 teams in their division advance to playoffs.

Skill Runs

  • One-minute trials focused on pure robot capability and scoring speed.
  • Manual & Autonomous: Separate rankings for driver proficiency and autonomous coding.
  • Rankings are determined by the sum of the team's best three runs in each category.
